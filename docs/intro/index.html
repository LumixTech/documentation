Introductionâ€‹

This portal organizes our engineering study notes into a maintainable Docusaurus documentation set. It is designed for internal learning, onboarding, architecture alignment, and future blog adaptation.

Why It Mattersâ€‹

Complex product delivery requires shared understanding across security, backend, frontend, data, and compliance domains. This documentation is structured to explain both implementation direction and decision rationale.

Core Conceptsâ€‹

  • Architecture decisions are connected across layers.
  • Security and compliance are product requirements, not afterthoughts.
  • Database and frontend patterns should remain policy-driven.

Problem in Real Product Developmentâ€‹

When knowledge lives only in chat threads or fragmented notes, teams lose context and repeat mistakes. A structured docs portal prevents decision drift.

Approach / Designâ€‹

  • Glossary

Sector Standard / Best Practiceâ€‹

  • Keep documents scoped but cross-linked.
  • Preserve decision history alongside technical details.
  • Standardize terminology and policy language.
